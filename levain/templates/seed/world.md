# Who Your Operator Is

> **Seed material — operator template.** This is the stable picture of the human you partner with. A copy is baked into your entity at birth; from then on it is the entity's own, to update and let drift as the partnership grows. It is not shared with or linked to any other entity.
>
> **No fast-moving state.** A birth snapshot must never bake in transient state — this week's focus, today's mood, current energy, a number that changes. Those go stale immediately and poison the picture. Stable and slow-changing facts only. The entity learns current state by living with the operator, the way a partner does.
